# Space Fighters - Complete Control Guide

## Full Mouse Support ✓

Space Fighters can be played entirely with the mouse! Every screen and action supports mouse controls.

---

## Splash Screen
- **Click anywhere** or press any key to skip

---

## Level Map Screen
- **Click on level circles** to select a level
- **Click again** on selected level to start
- **Arrow Keys** (alternative): LEFT/RIGHT to navigate levels
- **SPACE** (alternative): Confirm selection

**Visual Indicators:**
- GREEN circle = Completed level
- YELLOW circle = Selected level
- WHITE circle = Available level
- GRAY circle = Locked level

---

## Ship Selection Screen
- **Click on ships** to select
- **Click again** on selected ship to confirm
- **Arrow Keys** (alternative): LEFT/RIGHT to navigate ships
- **SPACE** (alternative): Confirm selection

**Visual Indicators:**
- YELLOW border = Selected ship
- Darkened + Lock icon = Locked ship
- Shows unlock requirements for locked ships

---

## Message Screens (Level Start, Boss Defeated, etc.)
- **Click the button** to continue
- **SPACE** (alternative): Continue

---

## Game Over / Level Complete Screens
- **Click "Restart" button** (green) to return to level map
- **Click "Quit" button** (red) to exit game
- **R key** (alternative): Restart
- **Q key** (alternative): Quit

---

## In-Game Controls

### Movement (Choose your style):
1. **Full Mouse Control:**
   - Hold **RIGHT MOUSE BUTTON** and move mouse to drag your ship
   - Hold **MIDDLE MOUSE BUTTON** (alternative) to drag your ship
   
2. **Keyboard Control:**
   - **WASD** or **Arrow Keys** to move
   
3. **Hybrid Control:**
   - Use keyboard for movement + mouse for shooting
   - Keyboard movement takes priority over mouse dragging

### Shooting:
- **LEFT MOUSE BUTTON** - Click or hold to shoot
- **SPACEBAR** (alternative) - Hold to shoot

### Best Mouse-Only Setup:
- **Right Mouse Button** (hold and drag) = Move ship
- **Left Mouse Button** (click/hold) = Shoot
- This allows full control with just the mouse!

---

## Control Combinations

### Mouse Only:
- Right button to move + Left button to shoot = Full mouse control

### Keyboard Only:
- WASD/Arrows to move + Spacebar to shoot = Full keyboard control

### Hybrid (Recommended):
- WASD to move + Left click to shoot = Precise movement + quick shooting
- Right mouse to move + Spacebar to shoot = Smooth movement + continuous fire

---

## Tips for Mouse Players

1. **Smooth Movement**: Hold right mouse button and your ship will smoothly follow the cursor
2. **Quick Shooting**: Tap left mouse button for single shots, hold for continuous fire
3. **Navigation**: All menus support clicking - no keyboard needed
4. **Level Selection**: Click directly on level circles in the map
5. **Ship Selection**: Click on ships to select and confirm

---

## Accessibility

- **Full mouse support** - Play without touching the keyboard
- **Full keyboard support** - Play without using the mouse
- **Flexible controls** - Mix and match as you prefer
- **Visual feedback** - Hover effects and highlights show clickable areas
- **Large click targets** - Easy to click buttons and selections

---

Every aspect of Space Fighters supports mouse control:
